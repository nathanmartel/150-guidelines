150th Anniversary Artwork

Modern Retro

Loyola’s anniversary mark embraces the central symbol of a nearly 500-year-tradition of excellence and care: the wolf and kettle. This version is confident and contemporary—a reflection that even as we celebrate 150 years of achievement, our efforts today are pointed forward.

Primary Celebration Mark

  • In most instances, the artwork should be contained in a circle to help maintain consistency and to aid legibility through Loyola channels.
  • In all instances, the affiliation to “Loyola University Chicago” or “Loyola Chicago” must be clear. This is usually accomplished by employing a University lockup.
  • Do not use smaller than a half-inch wide.
